Diet

Proper diet

Calories

To lose weight, most people need to reduce the number of calories they get from food and beverages (energy IN) and increase their physical activity (energy OUT).Very low calorie diets of fewer than 800 calories per day should not be used unless you are being monitored by your doctor.

Healthy Eating plan

A healthy eating plan gives your body the nutrients it needs every day while staying within your daily calorie goal for weight loss. A healthy eating plan also will lower your risk for heart disease and other health conditions.
